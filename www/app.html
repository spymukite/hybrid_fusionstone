Do it yourself masonry guide

Fusion Stone is an easy and budget-friendly way to beautifully upgrade your home, inside and out. What you need

Do it yourself masonry

You can actually do yourself!

  • tape measure
  • level
  • rubber head hammer
  • power drill and bit (bit is provided)
  • grinder or saw with masonry blade
  • safety glasses
  • breathable weather resistant barrier
  • plywood or OSB

Installation

Step 1

PREPARE YOUR SURFACE

Fusion Stone is installed over a Plywood or OSB substrate. Ensure that the substrate has been covered in a code approved, breathable, weather resistant barrier. Not required on interior installations.

Step 2

INSTALLING THE STARTER STRIP

Starter Strips are used at the base of all jobs as well as above any opening (windows and doors). Level and install the Starter Strips using the stainless steel screws provided.

Step 3

INSTALLING FUSION CORNERS

Begin installing Fusion Stone at the corner. As you build thecorner ensure that you alternate short and long sides.

Step 4

COMPLETE INSTALING STONE

Continue to lay the stone in the pattern described in the detailed instructions. The last stone installed on every row may need to be cut to length.

“Dry Stack” Fusion Stone derives its name from the innovative feature of no mortar installation that translates into the much valued benefit of an easy, no mess application.
